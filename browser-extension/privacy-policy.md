# Privacy Policy for Min-Conflit Solver

Last updated: 2026-05-20

Min-Conflit Solver runs its solving logic locally inside the browser.

## What the extension stores

The extension may store the following data locally on your device:

- your selected mode (`N-Queens` or `Sudoku`)
- your selected solver settings
- your last custom Sudoku grid
- a small summary of the last solver run

This local storage is used only to restore your preferences and improve continuity between sessions.

## What the extension does not collect

The extension does not:

- collect browsing history
- read the content of websites
- transmit personal data to a remote server
- sell or share personal data with third parties

## Remote services

The extension does not require a remote backend for its core functionality.
